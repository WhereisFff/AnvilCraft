# Canned Food

<item id="anvilcraft:canned_food"/>

~~Quick and easy eating~~

- Craft a Tin Can with any food to obtain Canned Food
- Eating canned food does not return the Tin Can
- When crafting Canned Food, you can use multiple of the same food together, up to five
  - One food: 100%
  - Two food: 180%
  - Three food: 240%
  - Four food: 280%
  - Five food: 300%
