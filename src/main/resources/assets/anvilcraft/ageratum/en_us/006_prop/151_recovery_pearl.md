# Recovery Pearl

<row halign="center">
<recipe id="anvilcraft:recovery_pearl"/>
</row>


- Right-click to use; teleports the player to their last death point
- If the player is within 12 blocks of their last death point, right-clicking will teleport them to their respawn point
- Consumed upon use and deals 4 points of fall damage to the player
