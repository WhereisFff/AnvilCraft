# Filter

<recipe id="anvilcraft:filter"/>

# Function


Hold a Filter and right-click to open its GUI, or right-click a Filter item in another UI to open its GUI:

- The 18 slots on the right are where items are placed; placing items does not consume them, only leaves a marker
- The two slots on the left are:
  - A button to "toggle whether to match NBT data"
  - A button to "toggle whitelist/blacklist"

Can be placed into machines with filter slots; once placed, that slot can hold all items matching the filter's criteria

<info>
Machines with filter slots include <ref item="anvilcraft:chute"/> <ref item="anvilcraft:magnetic_chute"/> ...
</info>
