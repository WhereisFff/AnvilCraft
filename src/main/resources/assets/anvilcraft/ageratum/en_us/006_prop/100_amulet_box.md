# Amulet Box

<row halign="center">
<item id="anvilcraft:amulet_box"/>
</row>

# Acquisition

- Can be obtained by trading with a master-level [Jeweler](../001_feature/001_jeweler.md) villager

---

# Interaction

- Similar to a Bundle, can hold Totems of Undying or Amulets
- Hold the Amulet Box and right-click to store Totems of Undying from your inventory into it
- Hold the Amulet Box and sneak + right-click to remove all Totems of Undying

---

- Each Totem of Undying occupies 1 slot in the Amulet Box
- Each basic Amulet occupies 2*3, i.e., 6 slots
- Hover your cursor over the Amulet Box in your inventory to view its occupancy

---

# Function

- Holding an Amulet Box containing a Totem of Undying in your offhand is equivalent to holding a Totem of Undying
- If you survive specific fatal damage, there is a 20% chance to obtain the corresponding [Amulet](100_amulet.md#获取). If it fails, the chance increases by an additional 10%
- When holding the Amulet Box in your main hand or offhand, all amulets inside are active
