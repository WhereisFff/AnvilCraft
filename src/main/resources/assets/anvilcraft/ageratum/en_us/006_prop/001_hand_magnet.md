# Hand Magnet

<recipe id="anvilcraft:magnet"/>

## Function

- Right-click to use; attracts nearby **dropped items** and **experience orbs** to your feet
- Shift + right-click to create/remove a **Magnetized Node** on the surface of a block

<info>
Can be enchanted with Mending, Unbreaking, and Curse of Vanishing
</info>

# Magnetized Node

<entity id="anvilcraft:magnetized_node" />

- Can only attach to the surface of blocks
- As soon as items touch the **attached block**, they are attracted to the **Magnetized Node** position
- Does not re-attract processed products, making it convenient for [Item Processing](../007_struct/000_item_processing.md)
- Use an item on the **attached block** while holding it to place the item on the **Magnetized Node**
