## Totem of Recovery

- Similar to a Totem of Undying, triggers when the player takes fatal damage (but also works against void damage); upon consumption, gives the player a Recovery Pearl item
- When consumed, grants the same potion effects as a Totem of Undying, teleports the player to their current respawn point, and sets the player's death location to the pre-teleport position; using a Recovery Pearl afterwards can return the player to the pre-teleport location
