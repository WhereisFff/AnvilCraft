# Uses

## Power Generation

- <ref item="anvilcraft:negative_matter_block"/> contains abundant void energy and can enhance the power generation capacity of <ref item="anvilcraft:void_energy_collector"/>:
  - For <ref item="anvilcraft:void_energy_collector"/>, each Negative Matter within the detection range offsets the effect of matter
  - Excess Negative Matter can increase power generation:
    - 11 or more: 1024kW
    - 21 or more: 2048kW
    - 41 or more: 4096kW

> <ref item="anvilcraft:negative_matter_block"/> is relatively stable and can be used together with <ref item="anvilcraft:void_matter_block"/> to fill the generator's detection range,
> preventing the generation of matter blocks and creating a maintenance-free generator structure
