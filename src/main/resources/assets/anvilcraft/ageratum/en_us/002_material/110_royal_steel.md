- As you can see, Royal Steel production can be boosted with Amethyst
- Royal Preference: Based on the world seed, one [gem](000_gems.md) other than Emerald is randomly selected. When that gem is used in Royal Steel synthesis, the yield is doubled!

<warning>
Note: Royal Preference cannot be viewed through JEI; it can only be discovered through experimentation
</warning>
