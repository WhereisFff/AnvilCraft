## <ref item="anvilcraft:neutronium_ingot"/>
### Crafting

1. Place any metal block/ingot/nugget on top of <ref item="anvilcraft:space_overcompressor"/>
2. Crush it in with an anvil to convert it into mass value
3. When <ref item="anvilcraft:space_overcompressor"/> accumulates enough mass value, it outputs one <ref item="anvilcraft:neutronium_ingot"/> below

### Properties

- Extremely dense; ordinary blocks are passed through as if they were air, but it is blocked by Bedrock, End Stone, End Dust, and other blocks containing Levitation Powder
