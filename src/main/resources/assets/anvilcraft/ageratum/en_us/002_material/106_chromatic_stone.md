# Molten Gems

<recipe id="anvilcraft:super_heating/melt_gem_cauldron_from_gem_block"/>

Place any [Gem Block](000_gems.md) into a cauldron and perform [Super Heating](../004_block/100_heater.md) to obtain a cauldron filled with molten gems


## Properties

- When the source block contacts water, it turns into <ref item="anvilcraft:chromatic_stone"/>
- When the flowing portion contacts water, it turns into <ref item="minecraft:granite"/>, <ref item="minecraft:diorite"/>, or <ref item="minecraft:andesite"/>
