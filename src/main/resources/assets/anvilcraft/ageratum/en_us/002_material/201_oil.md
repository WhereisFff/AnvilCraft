# Crude Oil

## Crafting

- The crude oil in this mod does not generate naturally; it is produced using special methods

<recipe id="anvilcraft:time_warp/oil_from_foods/raw_porkchop"/>

- Any meat can be used to refine oil
- One layer of crude oil (250mB) can be converted from the following materials:
  - 1x Zombie Head or Piglin Head
  - 16x <ref item="minecraft:beef"/> <ref item="minecraft:porkchop"/> <ref item="minecraft:mutton"/> <ref item="minecraft:rabbit"/>
  - 64x <ref item="minecraft:cod"/> or other fish, <ref item="minecraft:chicken"/> <ref item="minecraft:rotten_flesh"/> <ref item="minecraft:spider_eye"/>

## Uses

- Serves as fuel for the [Oil Plasma Generator](../007_struct/201_plasma_jets.md)

# Burning Oil Cauldron

## Production

- Ignite a cauldron containing crude oil using the following methods:
- Add <ref item="minecraft:torch"/> <ref item="minecraft:campfire"/> <ref item="minecraft:blaze_powder"/> (consumed)
- Use <ref item="minecraft:flint_and_steel"/> <ref item="minecraft:fire_charge"/> or Flame arrows
- Add a [High-Temperature Block](../001_feature/101_heated_block.md) (not consumed)

## Uses

- Crafting <ref item="anvilcraft:ember_metal_ingot"/>
- Building the [Oil Plasma Generator](../007_struct/201_plasma_jets.md)
