# Acquisition

- Has a chance to spawn in the chest of a village Weaponsmith's house
- Trade with a Journeyman-level [Jeweler](../001_feature/001_jeweler.md)

# Use

Used to upgrade items or blocks to the [Royal Steel tier](110_royal_steel.md)
