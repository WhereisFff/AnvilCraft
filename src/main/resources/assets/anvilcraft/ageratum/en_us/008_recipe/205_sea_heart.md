# Mass Production of Heart of the Sea

<info>
Requires prior [Mass Production of <ref item="anvilcraft:sapphire"/>](204_gem.md) and [Mass Production of Sponge](002_sponge_gemmule.md)
</info>

<recipe id="anvilcraft:procedural_process/ancient_sea_reef"/>

Mining <ref item="anvilcraft:ancient_sea_reef"/> drops the following items:

- 1 <ref item="minecraft:heart_of_the_sea"/> +10% (10% chance for an additional Heart of the Sea, affected by Fortune, +10% per level)
- 4 <ref item="minecraft:prismarine_shard"/>
- 4 <ref item="minecraft:prismarine_crystals"/>
- 1 of each coral type
