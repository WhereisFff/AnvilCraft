# Mass-Producing <ref item="minecraft:trial_key"/>

Enter the trial chamber, challenge the monsters within, and earn generous rewards, including a <ref item="minecraft:trial_key"/>, then duplicate them using the <ref item="anvilcraft:jewelcrafting_table"/>

<recipe id="anvilcraft:jewel_crafting/trial_key"/>

# Resetting Vaults and Mass-Producing Trial Rewards

<row halign="center">
<item id="minecraft:vault"/>
</row>

Use <ref item="anvilcraft:lead_block"/> to make <ref item="minecraft:vault"/> forget it has ever been opened

<structure id="../structures/vault.snbt"/>

Then repeatedly use <ref item="minecraft:trial_key"/> on a <ref item="minecraft:vault"/> to obtain a large number of rewards, with a chance to get <ref item="minecraft:ominous_bottle"/>

# Mass-Producing <ref item="minecraft:ominous_bottle"/>

This step can be done through a pillager outpost, but the <ref item="anvilcraft:jewelcrafting_table"/> provides a more convenient duplication method

<recipe id="anvilcraft:jewel_crafting/ominous_bottle"/>

<tip>
The required <ref item="minecraft:experience_bottle"/> can be obtained by scooping from a cauldron or fish tank containing [experience fluid](../002_material/004_exp_gem.md) with a glass bottle
</tip>

# Mass-Producing <ref item="minecraft:ominous_trial_key"/>

- Consume a <ref item="minecraft:ominous_bottle"/> to put trial spawners into hard mode, challenge stronger monsters, and earn even more generous rewards, including a <ref item="minecraft:ominous_trial_key"/>
- <ref item="minecraft:ominous_trial_key"/> can also be mass-produced using the <ref item="anvilcraft:jewelcrafting_table"/>

<recipe id="anvilcraft:jewel_crafting/ominous_trial_key"/>

Use a <ref item="minecraft:ominous_trial_key"/> to open the corresponding-tier <ref item="minecraft:vault"/> and obtain a <ref item="minecraft:heavy_core"/>
