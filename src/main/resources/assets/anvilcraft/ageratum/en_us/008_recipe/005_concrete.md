# Cement

<recipe id="anvilcraft:bulging/cement_cauldron"/>

- The cement produced defaults to gray. Dropping dye into the cauldron and striking with an anvil can change the color.

# Concrete Processing

- Into a cement cauldron, drop 4 * <ref item="minecraft:gravel"/> + 4 * <ref item="minecraft:sand"/> and strike with an anvil to obtain 16 * Concrete, with the color determined by the cement.
- Into a cement cauldron, drop 2 * <ref item="minecraft:gravel"/> + 2 * <ref item="minecraft:sand"/> + 8 * <ref item="minecraft:iron_bars"/> and strike with an anvil to obtain 16 * Reinforced Concrete, with the color determined by the cement.

# Reinforced Concrete

- Good blast resistance
- Can withstand Creeper explosions (non-charged)
- Cannot fully withstand TNT explosions
