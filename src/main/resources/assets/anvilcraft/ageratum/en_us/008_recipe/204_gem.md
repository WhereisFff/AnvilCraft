# Converting Gems

1. You can manually trade with villagers to obtain some <ref item="minecraft:emerald_block"/>
2. Place the gem in a cauldron filled with molten gem and perform [Time Warping](../004_block/200_corrupted_beacon.md), consuming 1000mB of molten gem to produce the corresponding gem block

# Mass Production of Emeralds

- After obtaining the <ref item="anvilcraft:giant_anvil"/>, you can [craft spawners](210_spawner.md)
- Obtain a Vindicator spawner
- Kill Vindicators to obtain <ref item="minecraft:emerald"/>

# Automation

- Although Vindicators only drop <ref item="minecraft:emerald"/> when killed by a player
- We can still use <ref item="anvilcraft:ember_anvil"/> to perform [Anvil Looting](../001_feature/000_anvil_loot.md) on them, automating the production of <ref item="minecraft:emerald"/>
