# Sugar Block

<recipe id="anvilcraft:sugar_block"/>

# Function

- When struck by an anvil, generates 1 [charge](../003_power/100_basic_power_generation.md#charges)
- When struck, has a 5% chance to crack once; after 4 cracks, breaks into 9 sugar

# Properties

- Mineable with wooden pickaxe
- Drops itself when mined
