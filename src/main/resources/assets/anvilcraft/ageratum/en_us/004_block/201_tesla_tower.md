# Tesla Tower
<recipe id="anvilcraft:tesla_tower"/>

# Function

- Every 4 seconds, zaps the nearest mob or <ref item="minecraft:lightning_rod"/> within a radius of 8 blocks, dealing 10 damage
- A whitelist can be set in the GUI

---

## Properties

- Consumes 128kW of power when working; cannot work when power is insufficient
- Can be disabled by redstone signal
