Can scan structures within a customizable range

## Manual Save

Open the GUI:
1. Click the button on the right to scan the structure
2. Insert a structure disk
3. (Optional) Enter a structure name
4. Click the confirm button on the left to save

## Auto Save

1. Structure disks can be input/output via chutes and other logistics blocks
2. Upon receiving a redstone signal, executes a save
