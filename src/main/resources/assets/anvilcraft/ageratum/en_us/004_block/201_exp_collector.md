# <ref item="anvilcraft:exp_collector"/>
<row halign="center">
<recipe id="anvilcraft:exp_collector"/>
</row>

- Similar to <ref item="anvilcraft:item_collector"/>, collects experience orbs over a range
- Stores experience in the form of [Experience Fluid](../002_material/004_exp_gem.md)
- Can right-click the block with a bucket or right-click the experience fluid in the GUI to directly fill one bucket of experience fluid
