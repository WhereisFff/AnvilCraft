# Fish Tank

<recipe id="anvilcraft:fish_tank"/>

# Anvil Processing

- Can store up to 1000mB of fluid (one bucket), with 8 input slots and 8 output slots
- Right-click with an item to insert it
- Replaces the cauldron for related anvil crafting
- Right-click the side with an anvil hammer to turn it into a fish tank with an output port, which automatically outputs processed items

# Other Functions

<item id="minecraft:water_bucket"/>

- Special: Right-click the bottom half of the fish tank with a tropical fish bucket or water bucket to insert/remove tropical fish
- Can be worn on the head to gain temporary Water Breathing effect
