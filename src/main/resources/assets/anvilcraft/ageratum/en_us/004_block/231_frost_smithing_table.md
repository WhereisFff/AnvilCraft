# Frost Smithing Table

<recipe id="anvilcraft:smithing/frost_smithing_table"/>

## Function

- A special smithing table that does not consume any smithing templates
- Usually needs to be used with <ref item="anvilcraft:permutation_smithing_template"/> or <ref item="anvilcraft:deformation_smithing_template"/>

# Permutation Smithing

<recipe id="anvilcraft:permutation_smithing_template"/>

Preserves the item's form, converting its material

- Consume <ref item="anvilcraft:royal_steel_ingot"/> to convert between diamond tools/weapons and royal steel tools/weapons
- Consume <ref item="anvilcraft:ember_metal_ingot"/> to convert between netherite tools/weapons and ember metal tools/weapons
- Consume <ref item="anvilcraft:multiphase_matter"/> to convert between frost metal tools/weapons and ember metal tools/weapons
- Consume <ref item="anvilcraft:multiphase_matter_block"/> to convert between frost anvil/smithing table/grindstone and ember anvil/smithing table/grindstone
- Consume <ref item="anvilcraft:chromatic_stone"/> to convert between the four types of [Gem Amulets](../006_prop/100_amulet.md)

# Deformation Smithing

<recipe id="anvilcraft:deformation_smithing_template"/>
Preserves the item's material, converting its form

Consume <ref item="anvilcraft:frost_metal_ingot"/> to convert between the following tool forms:
- Sword, Axe, Pickaxe, Hoe, Shovel
- Helmet, Chestplate, Leggings, Boots
- Bow, Crossbow
