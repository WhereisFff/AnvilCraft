# 10-Trial of Ice and Fire

# <ref item="anvilcraft:frost_metal_ingot"/>

## Crafting

1. Obtain some powder snow — try using [the methods provided by this mod](../008_recipe/110_ice.md)
2. Produce some <ref item="anvilcraft:frost_metal_ingot"/>

## Usage

- Craft and try out frost metal tools — they can convert enchantments into pure stat values
- Craft the [Frost Smithing Table](../004_block/231_frost_smithing_table.md), <ref item="anvilcraft:frost_grindstone"/>, and <ref item="anvilcraft:frost_anvil"/>
- Use the properties of the <ref item="anvilcraft:frost_anvil"/> to farm experience orbs, then collect them with an <ref item="anvilcraft:exp_collector"/>, and solidify them into <ref item="anvilcraft:exp_gem"/> for yourself or villagers

<tip>
For obtaining liquid experience, you can use the <ref item="anvilcraft:frost_anvil"/> property mentioned above, or build a mob farm and other traditional methods to obtain large amounts of experience orbs, then collect them with an <ref item="anvilcraft:exp_collector"/>
</tip>

# <ref item="anvilcraft:ember_metal_ingot"/>

## Crafting

1. [Produce some netherite](../008_recipe/210_netherite_ingot.md)
2. Produce <ref item="anvilcraft:oil_bucket"/> and obtain a *burning cauldron*
3. Produce some <ref item="anvilcraft:ember_metal_ingot"/>

## Usage

- Craft and try out ember metal tools — they can self-repair using the power of fire
- Craft an <ref item="anvilcraft:ember_anvil"/>, <ref item="anvilcraft:ember_grindstone"/>, and <ref item="anvilcraft:ember_smithing_table"/>
- Craft a wither cage: ember metal cannot be destroyed by the Wither, allowing you to trap it for safe killing

# Side Quest: Trial Chamber Rampage

- Obtain powerful equipment from the previous two materials, then enter the trial chamber to obtain materials
- This mod provides [many conveniences](../008_recipe/205_mace.md)
- Eventually, you can [mass-produce the mace](../008_recipe/205_mace.md)

# Side Quest: Ancient City

- Search chests in the ancient city, or have the [Warden kill mobs](../001_feature/101_echo_shard.md) (including yourself) to obtain <ref item="minecraft:echo_shard"/>

# Side Quest: Underwater Treasure

- Find treasure maps in shipwrecks, dig to obtain <ref item="minecraft:heart_of_the_sea"/>
- Wear a <ref item="anvilcraft:fish_tank"/> on your head for convenient diving
- Visit ocean monuments to obtain <ref item="minecraft:sponge"/>
- [Mass-produce <ref item="minecraft:sponge"/>](../008_recipe/002_sponge_gemmule.md), then [mass-produce <ref item="minecraft:heart_of_the_sea"/>](../008_recipe/205_sea_heart.md)
- [Mass-produce <ref item="minecraft:trident"/>](../008_recipe/205_sea_heart.md)
