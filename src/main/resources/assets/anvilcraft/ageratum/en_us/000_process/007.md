# 7-Corrupted Beacon

# Obtaining Cursed Gold

- Use gold ingots (or blocks) on a <ref item="anvilcraft:royal_grindstone"/> to purge curse enchantments or enchantment penalties, obtaining <ref item="anvilcraft:cursed_gold_ingot"/>

<tip>
Obtain cursed enchantment books through villager trading to mass-produce cursed gold
</tip>

<tip>
Items duplicated by the <ref item="anvilcraft:jewelcrafting_table"/> carry the Curse of Vanishing, which can be used to mass-produce cursed gold
</tip>

<tip>
Build an enderman farm or use other methods to gain experience, continuously repair wooden items to accumulate and then consume their enchantment penalties, which can be used to mass-produce cursed gold
</tip>

# Obtaining the Beacon

- Use the **Beheading** enchantment of the <ref item="anvilcraft:amethyst_sword"/> to quickly obtain <ref item="minecraft:wither_skeleton_skull"/>
- Summon the Wither and claim the <ref item="minecraft:nether_star"/>
- Craft a <ref item="minecraft:beacon"/>
