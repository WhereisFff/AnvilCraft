# 8-Giant Anvil

# Obtaining the <ref item="anvilcraft:giant_anvil"/>

Use the <ref item="anvilcraft:corrupted_beacon"/> to produce a <ref item="anvilcraft:giant_anvil"/>

# Tree Farm and Crop Farm

1. Utilize the [ground-shaking ability of the <ref item="anvilcraft:giant_anvil"/>](../004_block/210_giant_anvil.md), enable destruction mode, and automate tree chopping and crop harvesting. Crops will be automatically replanted.
2. Use a <ref item="anvilcraft:item_collector"/> to collect drops over a wide area
3. Use a <ref item="anvilcraft:block_placer"/> or <ref item="anvilcraft:smart_block_placer"/> to plant saplings
4. Use an <ref item="anvilcraft:induction_light"/> to accelerate sapling growth

<tip>
The guide book provides an [example machine](../009_machine/210_tree.md) that you can adjust based on your needs
</tip>

# Mass-Producing Diamonds

Using logs rich in carbon, you can [automate the mass production of <ref item="minecraft:diamond"/>](../008_recipe/201_diamond.md)!
