# 14-Stepping into Eternity

## Transcendium Alloy

1. Craft a <ref item="anvilcraft:charged_neutronium_ingot"/>
2. Smash nuclear fuel blocks to heat up an <ref item="anvilcraft:ember_metal_block"/> and obtain an <ref item="anvilcraft:overheated_ember_metal_block"/>
3. Quickly smash the <ref item="anvilcraft:charged_neutronium_ingot"/> into the <ref item="anvilcraft:overheated_ember_metal_block"/> to obtain transcendium alloy

## Smithing Template

1. Craft an <ref item="anvilcraft:eight_to_one_smithing_template"/> and apply multiple enchantments to it
2. Destroy the template to trigger [Template Dissociation](../004_block/221_ember_smithing_table.md#模板解离) and obtain the <ref item="anvilcraft:transcendium_upgrade_smithing_template"/>
