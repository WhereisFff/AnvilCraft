# 13-New Substances

# Neutronium Ingot

1. Craft a <ref item="anvilcraft:space_overcompressor"/> — this requires 8 <ref item="minecraft:shulker_shell"/>, you can try [mass-producing them](../000_process/009.md#mass-producing-shulker-shells)
2. After preparing a large amount of <ref item="anvilcraft:heavy_iron_block"/> (or other metals), compress them inside the <ref item="anvilcraft:space_overcompressor"/> to synthesize <ref item="anvilcraft:neutronium_ingot"/>


<tip>
Extremely iron-intensive; it is recommended to significantly upgrade your iron farm, for example by adding more repair dispensers and iron golems
</tip>

# Plutonium

1. Produce it using the <ref item="anvilcraft:neutron_irradiator"/>
2. Currently, [Plutonium](../002_material/321_plutonium.md) is mainly used for power generation
