# Converting Villagers & Wandering Traders

## Converting Villagers

<entity id="minecraft:villager" />

- When a villager is hit by an anvil (must not be killed), it will be reset
- There is a **20%** chance it becomes a Nitwit

## Converting Wandering Traders

<entity id="minecraft:wandering_trader" />

- When a Wandering Trader is hit by an anvil (must not be killed), it will be converted into a Villager
- There is a **15%** chance it becomes a Nitwit
- There is an additional **10%** chance it retains its previous trades (profession becomes Master-Farmer)
