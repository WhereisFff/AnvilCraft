# Dispenser Enhancements

## Cauldron Interaction

<structure id="../../structures/dispenser.snbt"/>

<ref item="minecraft:dispenser"/> can now use Water Bottles, Empty Bottles, Lava Buckets, Powder Snow Buckets, Water Buckets, and Empty Buckets to interact with <ref item="minecraft:cauldron"/>.

## Mob Interactions

- Dispense <ref item="minecraft:iron_ingot"/> to repair Iron Golems
- Dispense <ref item="minecraft:bucket"/> at Cows to collect milk
- Dispense <ref item="minecraft:bowl"/> at Mooshrooms to collect mushroom stew
- Dispense <ref item="minecraft:golden_apple"/> to cure weakened Zombie Villagers
