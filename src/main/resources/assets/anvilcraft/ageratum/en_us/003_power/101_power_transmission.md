# Power Transmission

Each transmission pole has its own power supply range. Generators and consumers within range connect to the same power grid. Two transmission poles connect if their power supply ranges have any overlap.

*Transmission pole*: Supply range 8, coverage area 17x17x17 centered on the head.

<recipe id="anvilcraft:transmission_pole"/>

*Remote transmission pole*: Supply range 16, coverage area 33x33x33 centered on the head.

<recipe id="anvilcraft:remote_transmission_pole"/>

*Load monitor*: Can check grid load and output a comparator signal proportional to the load. When overloaded, it also directly outputs a redstone signal.
