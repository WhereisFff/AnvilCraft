This chapter introduces the power system, including power generation and transmission

# Power System Introduction

- The *power grid* in this mod is wireless. Power transmission poles provide power coverage, connecting generators and consumers to the grid.
- Two overlapping *power grids* will merge, so you don't need to place generators in every *grid*. Simply insert a transmission pole at intervals to ensure that the generator's power covers all consumers through the *power grid*.
- Power in this mod is wattage-based. If the generation power in the *power grid* is greater than or equal to the consumption power, all consumers will work normally. Otherwise, the grid will overload and fail to function.
