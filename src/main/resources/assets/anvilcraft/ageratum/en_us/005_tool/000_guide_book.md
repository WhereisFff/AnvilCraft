# AnvilCraft Guide Book

<item id="anvilcraft:guide_book"/>


# Acquisition:

- A player receives an **AnvilCraft Guide Book** upon first entering a world

<recipe id="anvilcraft:guide_book"/>

# Functions:

1. **Hover** your mouse over the guide and press **W** to open AnvilCraft's "Integration", where you can view what addons AnvilCraft includes and what mods it has compat/integration with, allowing you to install them as needed
2. Use the guide directly in-game to view most AnvilCraft information
3. Press a shortcut key on certain items to directly open the guide and view item information

# Other:

1. The information provided by the guide may lag behind changes to the mod itself; it needs timely updates (remember to give feedback)
2. The guide's updates and improvements need your help; if you have the determination and ability, you can contribute to the guide
3. Some information cannot be provided by the guide; when necessary, consult the internet or ask other players
