## 怨念琥珀块

- [<translate key="block.anvilcraft.resin_block"/>](../004_prop/000_resin_block.md)可被[时移](../005_block/200_corrupted_beacon.md)为[<translate key="block.anvilcraft.amber_block"/>](../002_material/200_amber.md)
- 如果[<translate key="block.anvilcraft.resin_block"/>](../004_prop/000_resin_block.md)中含有**非被动生物**，则有 5% 的概率产生[<translate key="block.anvilcraft.resentful_amber_block"/>](210_spawner.md)
- 利用[<translate key="block.anvilcraft.resentful_amber_block"/>](210_spawner.md)和[<translate key="block.anvilcraft.cursed_gold_block"/>](../002_material/122_cruse_gold.md)，可以生产对应的<translate key="block.minecraft.spawner"/>
