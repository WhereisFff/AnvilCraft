# 流程14-迈入永恒

## 超限合金

1. 制作[<translate key="item.anvilcraft.charged_neutronium_ingot"/>](../002_material/221_neutronium_ingot.md)
2. 撞击核燃料块，加热[<translate key="block.anvilcraft.ember_metal_block"/>](../002_material/211_ember_metal.md)，获得[<translate key="block.anvilcraft.overheated_ember_metal_block"/>](../001_feature/301_overheated_block.md)
3. 迅速将[<translate key="item.anvilcraft.charged_neutronium_ingot"/>](../002_material/221_neutronium_ingot.md)砸入[<translate key="block.anvilcraft.overheated_ember_metal_block"/>](../001_feature/301_overheated_block.md)，就能获得超限合金

## 锻造模板

1. 制造[<translate key="item.anvilcraft.eight_to_one_smithing_template"/>](../002_material/213_multiple_template.md)，为其附上多种附魔
2. 摧毁模板，触发[模板解离](../002_material/213_multiple_template.md#模板解离)，获得[<translate key="item.anvilcraft.transcendium_upgrade_smithing_template"/>](../002_material/311_transcendium_template.md)

## 升级装备

- [超限合金升级](../002_material/312_transcendium.md)

<info>
超限装备永远不会损坏
</info>
